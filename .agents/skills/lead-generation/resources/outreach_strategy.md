# Outreach Strategy Guide

## Channel Priority by Region

### Southern & Eastern Europe (Croatia, Serbia, Greece, etc.)
1. 📞 **Phone call** (90%+ coverage) — highest response rate
2. 📘 **Facebook message** (30-40%) — very popular for business communication
3. 📸 **Instagram DM** (15-25%) — good for trendy/younger shops
4. 📧 **Email** (5-15%) — rare, only established businesses

### Western & Northern Europe (Germany, UK, Netherlands, etc.)
1. 📧 **Email** (40-60%) — more businesses have email
2. 📞 **Phone call** (85%+) — always reliable
3. 📸 **Instagram DM** (20-30%) — good for creative industries
4. 📘 **Facebook message** (20-30%) — less popular for B2B

---

## Lead Quality Classification

Use these categories to prioritize your outreach:

### 🔥 Hot Lead
**Criteria**: Has email + phone + website
**Priority**: Contact first
**Approach**: Send a professional email, then follow up by phone if no response in 48h

### 🟡 Warm Lead
**Criteria**: Has phone + at least one social media or website
**Priority**: Contact second
**Approach**: Call directly during business hours, or send a Facebook/Instagram message

### 🔵 Cold Lead
**Criteria**: Has phone only, no digital presence
**Priority**: Contact third
**Approach**: Call directly — these businesses likely prefer traditional communication

### ⚪ Minimal Lead
**Criteria**: No phone, no meaningful digital presence
**Priority**: Low — may be inactive or very small operations
**Approach**: Try visiting in person if in the area, or send a letter

## Outreach Best Practices

### Phone Calls (Croatian market)
- Call during **business hours** (check opening hours from scrape data)
- Speak in **Croatian** if possible, or have a Croatian speaker
- Keep it brief: introduce yourself, state value proposition in 30 seconds
- Reference their Google Maps rating as an ice-breaker
- Example: *"Vidim da imate odlične recenzije na Googleu..."* (I see you have excellent Google reviews...)

### Facebook Messages
- Message the **business page**, not personal profiles
- Include your business name and a clear call-to-action
- Attach a visual (product image, price sheet) for higher engagement
- Follow up after 3 days if no response

### Instagram DMs  
- Follow the business first, like 2-3 of their posts
- Wait 1-2 days before sending a DM
- Keep the message conversational and visual
- Business accounts have separate inbox — messages may go to "Requests"

### Email (when available)
- Use a clear subject line with their business name
- Keep the email under 150 words
- Include a single clear call-to-action
- Follow up once after 5-7 days
